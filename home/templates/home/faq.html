How do I place an order?

Placing an order is very easy. Just follow the steps below:

  1. Click on the product you want to buy.
  2. Click on the "Add to Cart" button.
  3. Click on the "Cart" icon at the top right corner of the page.
  4. Click on the "Checkout" button.
  5. Fill in your details and click on the "Place Order" button.
  6. Make payment.
  7. Wait for your order to be delivered.

How do I pay for my order?

How long will it take to receive my order?

It will take 3-5 business days to process your order. After that, it will take 7-10 business days for delivery in the United States, and 10-15 business days for international orders (depending on location).
